# 13.0 Conclusion

WIMMA Lab is a very intense project that leaves its mark on every participant.

Students that participated in WIMMA Lab in the summer of 2018, WIMMA Lab is a unique opportunity to gain practical experience of larger projects step by step, from start to finish. As it is not a real job, but a school simulated one, you can also afford to test new things in peace and to fail without any financial damage. WIMMA Lab offers numerous interesting projects, managerial experiences, and unique networking opportunities.

WIMMA Lab continues ahead, ever greater.
