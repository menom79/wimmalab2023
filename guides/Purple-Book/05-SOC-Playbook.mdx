# SOC Playbook 

Within the core of the SOC is the playbook, which functions as the guideline for all SOC activities. The playbook is something that is always a work in progress, and as such our playbook for Mysticons 2022 is not finished. You can read through our playbook from our OPF pages: 

https://wimma-lab-2022.pages.labranet.jamk.fi/mysticons/core/ 

Future Mysticons members should focus on implementing the playbook further, seeing what information is important and build upon that. The finalized product should be easy to read for everyone working at the Mysticons SOC.
